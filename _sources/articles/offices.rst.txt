OFFICES
#######

.. _offices_registered:
=================
REGISTERED OFFICE
=================

The registered office of the Corporation shall be at such location as established in the Certificate
of Incorporation, as the same may be amended from time to time.

.. _offices_other:
=============
OTHER OFFICES
=============

Additional offices and places of business located within and/or outside the State of Delaware
may be established from time to time by resolution of the Board duly adopted in accordance with
these Amended Bylaws.
