1. OFFICES¶

1.1. REGISTERED OFFICE¶

The registered office of the Corporation shall be at such location as established in the Certificate of Incorporation, as the same may be amended from time to time.

1.2. OTHER OFFICES¶

Additional offices and places of business located within and/or outside the State of Delaware may be established from time to time by resolution of the Board duly adopted in accordance with these Amended Bylaws.
